Tournament Details

⛳

The Course

Pickering Glen Golf Club, a beautiful 18-hole parkland course established in 1968. Located at 230 Concession Road 7, Pickering, Ontario.

🗓️

The Date

Wednesday, July 23, 2026. Shotgun start at 12:00pm. Dinner and awards in the evening.

🏆

The Format

Best ball scramble format. Teams of four. Prizes for longest drive, closest to the pin, and tournament champions.
