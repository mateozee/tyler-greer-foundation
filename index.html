About Tyler

Celebrating a Life
Well Lived

The Tyler Greer Foundation was established to honour the memory of Tyler Greer, whose warmth, energy, and love for life touched everyone around him.

This foundation carries forward Tyler's spirit by bringing people together through community, sport, and giving back. The First Annual Charity Golf Tournament is our way of celebrating Tyler's legacy while making a meaningful difference.

Every dollar raised goes directly to supporting the foundation's mission of community impact in Tyler's name.

About the Foundation

Our Mission

The Tyler Greer Foundation was created to honour Tyler's memory by giving back to the community he loved. Through charitable events, donations, and community initiatives, we aim to create lasting impact in Tyler's name.

100% of all funds raised go directly to supporting the foundation's mission. Whether through our annual golf tournament, direct donations, or community partnerships, every contribution helps keep Tyler's legacy alive.
